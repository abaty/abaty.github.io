Joining the UIC Physics faculty!

August 16, 2023

I am delighted to announce that I have just started a new position as an assistant professor at the University of Illinois Chicago! I will be starting a research group dedicated to examining high-density quantum chromodynamics by using the CMS detector to study heavy ion collisions at the LHC. I will also be involved in the development of a detector for the upcoming electron-ion collider. I look forward to cooperating with my new colleagues in the UIC Physics department and contributing to the lively and diverse atmosphere on campus!
